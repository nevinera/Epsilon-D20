Equipment List

Coinage

The most common coin is the gold piece (gp). A gold piece is worth 10 silvers, a silver is worth 10 coppers. Platinum is 10 gold.

Characters begin with some money (with which to buy their initial equipment):

  • Fighter: 150
  • Paladin: 130
  • Rogue: 125
  • Mage: 70
  • Cleric: 115
  • Druid: 60
